EMPIRICAL: THE REVELATIONS

Produced by Professor Phen

The Revelations is the Bass Creek Label's debut project rapped by Empirical and produced by Professor Phen. It strives to address social problems, poke fun at issues of life, and reminisce in the golden age of hip hop. The sounds are remniscence of 1980-2000 rap jazz and rap rock funk. The project is expected to be released ion late July/August.
